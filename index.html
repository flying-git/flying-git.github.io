Skylanders (A Franchise)

Hi there! If you found this page, maybe it's by accident. Either way, welcome to this fan-made introduction to Blizzard & Activision's game series "Skylanders". It's a decent franchise, utilizing the Toys to Life mechanic that had been introduced in 2007. When Skylanders first came out during 2011, its first game "Spyro's Adventure" involved the titular protagonist of another series: Spyro the Dragon. Ever since, there have been a total of 6 mainstream games, with side games to boot. Look below for more info.
